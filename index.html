Entrepreneurship In Technology Event

Technology is often viewed as tools we use to facilitate our lives. However, all democratized technologies initially relied on innovation and entrepreneurship to progress society. This event aims to give a feel of the vast opportunities that exist when technology is used for entrepreneurship. Below is the timeline for the event:

1:00 PM - 1:10 PM - Introduction
1:10 PM - 1:55 PM - Journey from Engineering to Entrepreneurship
1:55 PM - 2:40 PM - Dealing with Startup Failure
2:40 PM - 3:25 PM - Future Trends of Startups in Tech
3:25 PM - 3:35 PM - Closing Remarks

About Us

Who and why Ascent?

Ascent is a youth-led organization founded by high-school students from Ottawa, Ontario, which aims to expose young students to the expanding fields of the future. We allow students to learn about emerging technologies, (such as AI and augmented reality etc.) explore career options, and network with professionals; as well as take part in interactive workshops, challenges, and much more!!

Our aim is to provide the resources and platforms to jump start careers in becoming tomorrow’s intellectual pioneers. We hope to see you at one of our upcoming events soon!
